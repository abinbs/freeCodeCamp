A Brief Chronology of Dr. Ambedkar

  • 1896. Born in Maharastra
  • 1907. Qualifies for matriculation
  • 1913. Joined Columbia University (USA) with economics sociology as major subjects
  • 1922. Is awarded Bar-At-Law (Barrister) degrees by Greys-Inn, London
  • 1927. Launched ‘Mahad Satyagrah’ for human rights of the DCs
  • 1930. Launches ‘Kalaram Temple Satyagrah’ for “religious equality” and appeal to the conscience of caste Hindus
  • 1935. Wrote his Autobiographical notes Waiting for A Visa
  • 1947. become the first Law Minister of Independent India
  • 1949. Chairman for drafting the Constitution of India
  • 1956. Dr. Ambedkar demised
